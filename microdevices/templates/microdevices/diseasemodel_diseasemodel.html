    NAFLD Disease Models
    Overview
    The NAFLD Model Portal page is where you can go to find all information relevant to microphysiology systems models related to NAFLD. Here you can also find the studies associated with these models. Currently, the MPS Database is the warehouse for these models. Currently, the University of Pittsburgh is the only institution to submit a disease model for the NAFLD disease state.
